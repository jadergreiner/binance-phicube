# Layer Contract: Business Rules

## Mission

Define why the change exists and which business/domain rules must hold.

## Required Inputs

- Problem statement
- Desired outcome
- Stakeholders and constraints

## Required Outputs

- Business objective
- Domain rules
- Scope boundaries (in/out)
- Acceptance intent (business view)

## Quality Criteria

- Rule clarity and non-ambiguity
- Explicit non-goals
- Testable expected outcomes

## Blocking Criteria

- Missing expected outcome
- Contradictory domain rules
- Unclear scope boundaries
