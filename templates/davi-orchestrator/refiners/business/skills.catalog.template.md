# Skills Catalog Template: Business Rules

## Mandatory Skills (minimum set)

1. Domain Discovery

- Purpose: extract domain language, entities, and rules.
- Trigger: initial request has vague or mixed terms.

1. Scope Framing

- Purpose: define in-scope vs out-of-scope.
- Trigger: request can expand beyond intended objective.

1. Acceptance Modeling

- Purpose: convert value expectation into measurable acceptance intent.
- Trigger: no clear success definition exists.

## Optional Skills

1. Stakeholder Mapping

- Purpose: map impact by role/team.

1. Risk Framing (business)

- Purpose: identify business-side failure impact.
