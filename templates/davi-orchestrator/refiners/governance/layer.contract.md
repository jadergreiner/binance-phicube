# Layer Contract: Governance

## Mission

Guarantee policy alignment, risk controls, and decision traceability before coding.

## Required Inputs

- Approved architecture outputs
- Applicable policies/compliance rules
- Risk tolerance and audit expectations

## Required Outputs

- Governance checks list
- Risk register and controls
- Go/No-Go recommendation
- Decision log for auditability

## Quality Criteria

- Traceability from requirement to control
- Explicit ownership for controls
- Clear Go/No-Go rationale

## Blocking Criteria

- Missing critical control
- Unassigned control ownership
- No decision traceability
