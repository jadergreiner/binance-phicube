# Skills Catalog Template: Governance

## Mandatory Skills (minimum set)

1. Policy Mapping

- Purpose: map internal/external rules to this change.
- Trigger: change has compliance or governance impact.

1. Risk and Control Design

- Purpose: define controls for top failure modes.
- Trigger: medium/high impact change.

1. Decision Traceability

- Purpose: record rationale, owner, and approval path.
- Trigger: before handoff to executor.

## Optional Skills

1. Audit Readiness

- Purpose: structure evidence expected for audits.

1. Rollback Governance

- Purpose: define reversal criteria and responsibilities.
