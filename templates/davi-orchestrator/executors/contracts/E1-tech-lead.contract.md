# E1 Contract - Tech Lead (Orchestrator)

## Mission

Receive approved Stage 6 tasks, orchestrate executor sequence, and enforce gates.

## Required Inputs

- Approved SPEC and Tasks
- Stage approvals checklist
- Handoff checklist

## Required Outputs

- Execution plan per executor
- Assignment and handoff log
- Final integrated delivery summary

## Quality Criteria

- No executor starts without required input gate
- Scope remains aligned to approved Tasks
- Traceability from task to implementation evidence

## Blocking Criteria

- Missing approval artifacts
- Lint gate `FAIL` on required docs/code artifacts
- Scope conflict without human decision
