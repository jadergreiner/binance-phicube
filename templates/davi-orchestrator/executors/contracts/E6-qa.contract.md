# E6 Contract - QA

## Mission

Validate acceptance criteria, regressions, and evidence completeness before closure.

## Required Inputs

- Approved acceptance criteria
- Implementation evidence from E5
- Relevant logs/reports

## Required Outputs

- QA acceptance report
- Defects/gaps list (if any)
- Final go/no-go recommendation

## Quality Criteria

- Acceptance criteria evaluated one by one
- Regression risk explicitly assessed
- Evidence is reproducible and traceable

## Blocking Criteria

- Critical acceptance criterion failed
- Missing evidence for mandatory behavior
- High-risk regression without mitigation
