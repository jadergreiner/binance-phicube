# E2 Contract - Solutions Architect

## Mission

Review technical solution coherence before implementation and during major changes.

## Required Inputs

- Approved SPEC
- Approved Plan and Tasks
- Current architecture constraints

## Required Outputs

- Solution review report
- Architecture risk notes
- Accepted constraints for execution

## Quality Criteria

- Design adheres to approved scope
- Trade-offs are explicit
- Risks and mitigations are actionable

## Blocking Criteria

- Core design conflict with approved SPEC
- Missing critical architecture decision
- Unresolved high-risk dependency
